Put these files in this folder:

- resume.pdf              → your downloadable résumé (linked from the hero and contact section)
- img/<project>.png        → showcase screenshots
- img/<project>.mp4        → showcase clips (short, muted, looping works best)

Once a file exists, open content/showcase.js and swap the matching card's
media area to point at it (see the comment at the top of that file).
